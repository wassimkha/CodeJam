Setup:

1) Create a free account on Open AI
2) Copy your API key from Open AI's dashboard
3) Paste your key in the extension's settings
4) Enjoy!

How to Use:

-Select a word from any page to get its definition
-Select a question from any page to get its answer (question must end with "?")
-Click directly on the extension icon for a more detailed response.
